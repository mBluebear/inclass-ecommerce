*Tyrannosaurus* is the most **fierce** dinosaur that ever existed.

The T-Rex lived during the [Late Cretaceous Period](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Cretaceous).

## Features 

- Sharp Teeth
- Small Arms
- Thick Tail
